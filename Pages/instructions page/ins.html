Instructions

•The player has to survive the game by not allowing the bird to crash on the obstacles.
•When the bird passes each obstacles , the score increases by 1.
•When the player hits the obstacle . The score he scored will be displayed and the high score will be displayed.
•For single player game , the mission is to score higher than any score scored before.
•In case of two player game,the first player will play first then the other. The person who scores the highest is the winner.
